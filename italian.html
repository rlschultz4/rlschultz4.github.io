Here are some delicious Italian restaurants you can go to in New York City

Caffe Buon Gusto

Caffe Buon Gusto has been recognized outstanding Italian cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. They offer modern interpreations of classic Italian dishes and uses only high quality fresh ingredients. For more information visit: www.caffebuongusto.net

Bella Blu

Bella Blu is an upscale Italian cuisine restaurant where a wonderful team presents a high-quality menu and an enjoyable time. They are praised for their amazing interior, delicious pizza and its far from mediocre menu. For more information visit: www.bellablunyc.com
